KPOP Overview

In this part of this site, I will be introducing what KPOP is for those who have no idea about it. Also, I will be sharing my opinions on people who dislike kpop with an unreasonable reason. Keep scrolling if you want to know these.

KPOP - the shortened form of the words "KOREAN POP". This genre of music is very well-known all through out the world. It is because of the widespread fans. These artists that I mentioned, they made me smile. KPOP isn't bad, the people who think it is are.

Welcome to Our KPOP Site!

made by @kkmymm - Kymm Guadalupe of 9-Brebeuf A

I am Widmarc Kymm P. Guadalupe, the creator of this site. May I briefly introduce myself to my dear viewers. I am 15 of age and started getting into KPOP on the year 2016. As a kpop fan, I idolize many artists including IU, IVE, Seventeen, Treasure, VIVIZ, Twice, Blackpink, ITZY, Enhypen, STAYC, The Boyz, NCT, and many many more.

In this site you will be able to know more informations about groups you may know or may not know from my perspective.

SEVENTEEN

A 13-member boy group from PLEDIS Entertainment. They are known as the "Theater Kids of KPOP" and the "Most Synchronized KPOP Group".

ITZY

A 5-member girl group from JYP Entertainment. They are known for their girlboss concepts that make their fans fall for them even more.

NCT

A 23-member boy group from SM Entertainment. In the whole group, there are 4 units. Mainly, NCT U, NCT 127, NCT Dream, and WayV

STAYC

A 6-member girl group from HIGHUP Entertainment. They are known to have an all "no skip" album which means that all their songs are worth listening to until the end.

TWICE

A 9-member girl group from JYP Entertainment. Everybody knows TWICE, don't we? They are popular for their quirky yet fun songs and music videos. However, they can be cool and fancy too.

BLACKPINK

A 4-member girl group from YG Entertainment. They are the most well-known kpop girl group in the world, especially being part of one of the BIG 3 companies in kpop.

TREASURE

A 12-member boy group from YG Entertainment. They are known as the brother of ICON, 2NE1, and BLACKPINK.

ENHYPEN

A 7-member boy group from BELIFT LAB Entertainment. They were formed from a survival show called I LAND, in which various challenges like dancing and singing are made for participants to prove they can be an idol.

THE BOYZ

An 11-member boy group from IST Entertainment. They debuted with 12 members, however they are continuing their career now as an 11-member group.

IU

One of the biggest soloists in the kpop industry under EDAM Entertainment. Many artistst idolize her for her beauty and intense vocals.

IVE

A 6-member girl group from STARSHIP Entertainment. Even before their debut, many fans have already looked up to this girls, until now that they have officially debuted.

VIVIZ

A 3-member girl group from BPM Entertainment. This group consists of the maknae line (youngest members line) of the disbanded group, GFRIEND
